Senior Thesis Project

A digital photography series that explores the subtleties of a horse barn. This is part of my senior thesis project I completed at Connecticut College. This is an exploration of the finer details that make up a horse barn and a different angle of looking at a horse, not the usual powershot of it jumping over a jump. I wanted to capture the moments that might get overlooked.
