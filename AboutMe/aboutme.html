Hello!

My name is Molli and I am a designer with a passion for photography, installation art, and print making. Design solves problems whether it’s big or small. As a designer I use design thinking and research to find those solutions.

I am hard working, enthusiastic, love to learn, quick learner, and put all my energy into my work. I am always excited to meet new people whether it’s to collaborate on a project, talk about design, or just have a chat! Aside from my artwork, I love dancing and horseback riding. I love these activities because they keep me active and my mind engaged.

I graduated from Connecticut College ‘17 with a B.A. in both Art: Graphic Design and East Asian Studies, I have a Certificate IV in Graphic Design from Shillington Education in Sydney Australia ‘18, and a certificate in Building Websites from Scratch from Codecademy ‘18. This past year I worked as a graphic designer for ARA Group in their in-house graphic design studio.
